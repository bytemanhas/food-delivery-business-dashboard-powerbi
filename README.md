# food-delivery-business-dashboard-powerbi
This project presents a Food Delivery Business Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze operational performance, profitability, and customer satisfaction. The dashboard helps identify key factors affecting business performance such as delivery time, discounts, and customer ratings.

# 🍔 Food Delivery Business Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

---

## 🎯 Objective
The objective of this project is to:
- Analyze profitability across cities and restaurants
- Understand the impact of discount strategies on profit
- Evaluate delivery performance and customer satisfaction
- Identify key factors influencing business growth

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel (Data Preparation)
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset contains food delivery transaction data with the following fields:
- Order ID
- City
- Restaurant
- Delivery Time
- Discount
- Profit
- Customer Rating
- Order Date

---

## 📊 Key Features of Dashboard

### KPI Cards:
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Delivery Time
- Average Customer Rating

### Visualizations:
- Profit by City (Bar Chart)
- Profit by Restaurant Rating (Column Chart)
- Monthly Profit Trend (Line Chart)
- Discount Impact on Profit (Scatter Plot / Bar Chart)

### Interactive Filters:
- City
- Restaurant Rating
- Discount Range

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Higher discount levels negatively impact profitability
- Certain cities generate significantly higher profits
- Faster delivery time is associated with better customer ratings
- Most ratings fall within the mid-range, indicating scope for improvement

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Optimize discount strategies to avoid profit loss
- Improve delivery efficiency to enhance customer satisfaction
- Focus on high-performing cities and restaurants
- Monitor customer ratings to improve service quality
